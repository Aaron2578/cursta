## Overview

the biggest problem when it comes to learning anything new is our life is knowing what you know well and what you don't know. in other terms, keeping track of your progress while learning new skills in our life. this is problem I faced when I am preparating for my coding interviews. cursta ai will keep track of your progress and it tells you what are the topics and conscepts you are strong and you are weak.

## Features

- **Prepare based on company**: describe your dream company, job role, goals and your timeline. it creates a roadmap and keep track of your progress.
- **Mock interviews and reviews**: you can take a mock interview and get a feedback for every mock interviews.
- **free and easiest local setup**: you can easily setup this application locally and start use this applicaton for free with no cost.
- **Quality over Quantity**: instead of building 10 to 15 features, we are going to build the best progress tracking application which will surpass the quality of even a paid applications.

## Tech stack

- **Frameworks/libraries**: [React](https://react.dev/) + [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (app router)
- **Styling**: [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **UI Components**: [Shadcn](https://ui.shadcn.com/), [Aceternity](https://ui.aceternity.com/), [MagicUi](https://magicui.design/)
- **Backend (auth, database, storage)**: [Supabase](https://supabase.com/), [FastApi](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/)
- **Hosting**: [Vercel](https://vercel.com/)
- **ORM**: [Prisma](https://www.prisma.io/)
- **Data fetching**: [Tanstack Query](https://tanstack.com/)
